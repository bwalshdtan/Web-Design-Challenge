Summary: Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Cloudiness clustered around zero, 40%, 75%, and 100% acrosse latitudes. Desert areas (including the south pole) in certain latitudes that show low cloudiness offer a possible explanation for these clusters. Research has shown increased cloudiness over continents rather than oceans as well.
